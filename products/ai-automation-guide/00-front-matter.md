### How to Use This Book

This book is designed to be a working manual, not a theoretical treatise. Each chapter ends with an action checklist. The prompt templates in Chapter 10 are formatted so you can copy and paste them directly into ChatGPT, Claude, or any compatible AI tool.

Read chapters 1 through 3 in order. After that, jump to whichever chapter addresses your most pressing need.

The companion resources — templates, checklists, and calculators — are available in your product download folder.
