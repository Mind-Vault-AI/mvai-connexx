## Appendix A: Position Sizing Quick Reference Card

### The Formula

```
Position Size = (Account Balance x Risk %) / |Entry Price - Stop Loss Price|
```

### Quick Reference Table

For 1% risk on a EUR 10,000 account (EUR 100 risk per trade):

| Stop Distance | Position Size |
|--------------|--------------|
| EUR 0.50 | 200 units |
| EUR 1.00 | 100 units |
| EUR 2.00 | 50 units |
| EUR 5.00 | 20 units |
| EUR 10.00 | 10 units |
| EUR 25.00 | 4 units |
| EUR 50.00 | 2 units |

```
[ ] No major news in next _____ hours
[ ] Within my trading hours
[ ] Below maximum daily trades limit
[ ] Not recovering from daily loss limit

[ ] Emotional state: Calm and focused

[ ] Trade plan written in journal
[ ] Entry, stop, and target(s) documented
[ ] Management rules documented

ALL BOXES CHECKED? → EXECUTE
ANY BOX UNCHECKED? → DO NOT TRADE
```

## Appendix D: Recommended Reading

### Trading Psychology
- *Trading in the Zone* by Mark Douglas — The foundational text on trading psychology
- *The Daily Trading Coach* by Brett Steenbarger — 101 practical psychology lessons
- *Thinking in Bets* by Annie Duke — Decision-making under uncertainty
- *The Psychology of Money* by Morgan Housel — Behavioral finance principles

### Risk Management
- *The New Trading for a Living* by Dr. Alexander Elder — Complete trading system including risk management
- *Trade Your Way to Financial Freedom* by Van K. Tharp — Position sizing and R-multiples
- *Fooled by Randomness* by Nassim Taleb — Understanding probability and risk

### Performance Psychology
- *Mindset* by Carol Dweck — Fixed vs. growth mindset
- *Peak* by Anders Ericsson — The science of deliberate practice
- *The Checklist Manifesto* by Atul Gawande — Why checklists work under pressure
- *Thinking, Fast and Slow* by Daniel Kahneman — The two systems of decision-making

### Market Structure
- *Reminiscences of a Stock Operator* by Edwin Lefevre — Timeless market psychology
- *Market Wizards* by Jack Schwager — Interviews with top traders
- *Atomic Habits* by James Clear — Building sustainable behavioral change
