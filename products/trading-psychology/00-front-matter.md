## About This Book

This book exists because most traders fail for reasons that have nothing to do with their strategy.

The statistics are brutal: 70-90% of retail traders lose money. Not because they picked the wrong indicator or the wrong timeframe. They lose because they cannot manage their own psychology and their own risk.

A mediocre strategy with excellent risk management and emotional discipline will outperform a brilliant strategy with poor risk management every single time. This book gives you the psychological frameworks and risk management tools to become the exception to the statistic.

Every formula is practical. Every framework is battle-tested. Every chapter includes exercises you can implement in your next trading session.
